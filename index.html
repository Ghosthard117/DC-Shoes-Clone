At DC Shoes we turn beginners into legends, our brand represents a bunch of guys who have never left the skate park. A skate brand that can't keep up with the status quo, born on the street, creating awesome skate decks and apparel that can keep up with skaters. More than wood and wheels, we've upheld our skate credentials as the best skate brand for guys who like to spend their time in the park and the pros alike. We have incredible designs and streetwear for the most famous urban sport: skateboarding.

Swapping the wheels for the soft, white stuff, our snowboard-style attitude to altitude brings urban grind to the world's wildest places. Although DC Shoes was born in the skatepark, we also design skiwear and snowboardwear collections for the best snowboarders. The best snowboard brand for the city style with incredible styles, DC is the snowboard brand that mixes urban innovation with the style of the sky. Cutting through the hype in order to bring you the best performance with a playful edge, we've brought the same professionalism to our ski apparel by offering optimized precision in all of our ski and snowboard gear, for example ski backpacks and technical backpacks.
